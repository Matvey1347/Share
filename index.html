FAQ

What is Rapid Trading?
Rapid Trading is an advanced trading bot designed for the high-volatility memecoin market. It provides lightning-fast execution, automated strategies, and real-time market analytics across multiple blockchains.
How does Rapid Trading keep my funds secure?
Your private keys are encrypted and stored locally,  never shared with third parties. Rapid also integrates anti-scam filters to detect honeypots, low liquidity, and other risks before executing trades.
Which strategies can I use with Rapid?
You can set automated strategies including limit orders, stop-loss, take-profit, and grid trading. Rapid also allows real-time sniping of new token launches and tracking trending memecoins.
How can I access Rapid Trading?
Rapid is available through a sleek web dashboard and a Telegram bot for trading on the go. Simply connect your wallet, configure your strategy, and start trading at top speed.
